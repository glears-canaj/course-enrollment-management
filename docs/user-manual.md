# CourseManager — User Manual

## Section 1: Getting Started

### 1.1 Creating an Account
1. Navigate to the application home page.
2. Click the **Register** link to create a new account.
3. Fill in your **Full Name**, **Email**, and **Password** (minimum 6 characters).
4. Select your role from the dropdown menu (Student or Admin/Staff).
5. Click **Create Account**.

### 1.2 Logging In and Out
* **To Log In**: Enter your registered email and password on the login screen and click **Sign In**.
* **To Log Out**: Click the **Logout** button located in the top right corner of the navigation bar at any time.

---

## Section 2: Student Guide

### 2.1 Browsing the Catalog
Once logged in as a student, you will be directed to the **Browse Courses** screen. This page lists all courses currently offered by the university.

### 2.2 Searching and Filtering
You can narrow down the course list using the filter toolbar:
* **Search Box**: Type a course name or code (e.g., "CS101" or "Algebra") to instantly filter the list.
* **Credits**: Use the dropdown to show only courses worth a specific number of credits.
* **Schedule**: Type a day (e.g., "Monday") to find courses matching that schedule.
* **Available Seats**: Check the box to hide courses that are currently at maximum capacity.

### 2.3 Enrolling in a Course
1. Click on any course row in the catalog to view its details.
2. Review the instructor, schedule, and course description.
3. If seats are available, click the green **Enroll** button in the top right.
4. A success message will confirm your registration. 
*(Note: The button will turn grey and display "Course Full" if no seats are available).*

### 2.4 Viewing Your Enrollments
1. Click **My Enrollments** in the top navigation bar.
2. Here you will see a personalized list of all courses you have enrolled in, along with the date of your enrollment.

### 2.5 Unenrolling from a Course
To drop a class:
* Go to **My Enrollments** and click the red **Unenroll** link next to the specific course.
* Alternatively, view the Course Detail page for that class and click the red **Unenroll** button.

## Section 3: Admin/Staff Guide

### 3.1 Viewing the Dashboard
Upon logging in as an administrator, you are greeted by the Dashboard. This screen provides high-level statistics, including the total number of active courses across the university, total student enrollments, and how many courses have reached their maximum capacity.

### 3.2 Creating a Course
1. Click **Manage Courses** in the navigation bar.
2. Click the **New Course** button.
3. Fill out the course form (Code, Name, Instructor, Description, Credits, Max Capacity, and Schedule).
4. Click **Create Course**. The course is immediately available for students to browse.

### 3.3 Editing a Course
1. From the **Manage Courses** page, locate the course you wish to modify.
2. Click the **Edit** link next to the course.
3. Update the necessary details and click **Update Course**.

### 3.4 Deleting a Course
1. From the **Manage Courses** page, click the red **Delete** link next to a course.
2. Confirm the deletion in the browser prompt.
*(Note: You cannot delete a course if students are currently enrolled in it. You must remove them first).*

### 3.5 Viewing Enrolled Students
1. From the **Manage Courses** page, click the **Students** link next to a course.
2. This displays a roster containing the names, emails, and enrollment dates of every student in that class.

### 3.6 Removing a Student from a Course
1. While viewing a course roster (as described in 3.5), locate the student you need to drop.
2. Click the red **Remove** link on their row.
3. Confirm the action to forcibly unenroll them from the system.
